# Industry KPI Notes

Use these as prompts for evidence extraction, not hard requirements. Industry-specific materiality should override generic lists.

## Cross-Industry

- Human capital: engagement, turnover, retention, training hours/cost, leadership pipeline, diversity, pay equity, safety, absenteeism, productivity.
- Intellectual capital: R&D, patent quality, software/data assets, product pipeline, new-product revenue, AI/DX capability, standardization, brand investment.
- Social/relationship capital: NPS, customer retention, complaint rate, supplier audits, human rights due diligence, community impact, brand trust.
- Natural capital: GHG emissions, energy, water, waste, circularity, biodiversity, land use, nature dependencies/impacts.
- Governance: board skills, committee oversight, incentive links, risk appetite, internal controls, investor dialogue cycle.

## Manufacturing

- Quality defects, warranty cost, yield, automation, safety, supplier resilience, energy intensity, circular materials, product carbon footprint.

## Chemicals / Materials

- Green product revenue, hazardous substance management, process safety, R&D pipeline, recycling, Scope 3, water and biodiversity risk.

## Food / Consumer Goods

- Brand trust, health/nutrition outcomes, sustainable sourcing, agricultural supply chain, packaging circularity, food loss, customer loyalty.

## Technology / Communications

- Data centers energy, privacy/security, AI governance, service reliability, churn, ARPU, digital inclusion, software assets, talent scarcity.

## Financials

- Sustainable finance exposure, financed emissions, risk governance, customer trust, conduct risk, digital resilience, human capital productivity.

## Healthcare / Pharma

- R&D pipeline, access to medicine, safety/pharmacovigilance, trial ethics, intellectual property, supply resilience, trust and compliance.
